# [JLR-1234] Jaguar Vehicles in Vehicle List

Type: BUG

Description:

### Expected Behaviour

**GIVEN** I am on the My Vehicles Screen

**WHEN** My Vehicles Load

**THEN** I can **only** see Land Rover vehicles

### Actual Behaviour

**GIVEN** I am on the My Vehicles Screen

**WHEN** My Vehicles Load

**THEN** I see Land Rover and Jaguar vehicles
